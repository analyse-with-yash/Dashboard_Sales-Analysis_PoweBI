## Sales Dashboard Analysis (Power BI)

This project is an interactive Sales Dashboard built to turn raw business data into clear, meaningful insights. Instead of just looking at numbers, the dashboard helps understand what’s really happening behind the sales performance — where the business is growing, where it’s struggling, and where opportunities exist.

---

## Project Overview

In any business, data is everywhere — but insights are not.
This dashboard was created to bridge that gap by analyzing key performance indicators like sales, profit, orders, and regional trends.

It provides a simple yet powerful way to:

Monitor overall business health
Identify top-performing regions and cities
Understand customer buying behavior
Support smarter, data-driven decisions

---

## Key Metrics
Total Sales: 12M
Total Profit: 1.89M
Total Orders: 500
Total Quantity Sold: 2K
Average Order Value: 24.95K
Profit Margin: ~6.5%

---

## Key Insights
Sales are not consistent — they fluctuate across months, with strong peaks during mid-year and year-end periods.
Cities like Kolkata, Delhi, Mumbai, and Chennai are driving the majority of both sales and profit.
The East and North regions slightly outperform others, indicating stronger market presence.
Online orders surpass offline, showing a clear shift towards digital purchasing behavior.
Despite strong sales numbers, profit margins remain relatively low, highlighting a need for cost optimization.

## Features
📅 Monthly trend analysis for Sales, Profit, and Orders
🌍 Region-wise and city-level performance breakdown
